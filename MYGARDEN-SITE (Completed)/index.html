Some common questions
were often asked to us

My flowers are falling off
or dying?

This could be due to overwatering, underwatering, or insufficient light. Check your watering routine and ensure the plant is getting enough light.

What causes leaves to
become a pale?

Pale leaves often indicate nutrient deficiencies or excessive sunlight. Adjust watering, fertilize, and provide appropriate light conditions.

What causes brown
crispy leaves?

Brown crispy leaves are usually from underwatering or low humidity. Increase watering and humidity to help the plant recover.

How do I choose the plant?

Select a plant based on your home's light conditions, available space, and how much care you can provide.

How do I change the pots?

Gently remove the plant from its pot, loosen the roots, and repot it into a larger container with fresh soil. Water thoroughly after repotting.

Why are gnats flying
around my plant?

Gnats are attracted to moist soil and decaying matter. Reduce watering, improve soil drainage, and remove any debris to control them.
